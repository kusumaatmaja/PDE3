Finite Difference

Here, we explore the differences between the methods for numerically calculating the derivative of a function. Use the slider to change the value of dx used to calculate the derivative.

The finite difference schemes are given by:

  • Forward difference:
    $$\frac{df}{dx} \approx \frac{f(x+dx) - f(x)}{dx}$$
  • Backward difference:
    $$\frac{df}{dx} \approx \frac{f(x) - f(x-dx)}{dx}$$
  • Central difference:
    $$\frac{df}{dx} \approx \frac{f(x+dx) - f(x-dx)}{2dx}$$
